# Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) Alignment

This project directly aligns with five United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs):

## SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being
- **Target**: Reduce water-borne diseases and chemical contamination.
- **Implementation**: The water quality module aggregates pH, turbidity, TDS, DO, BOD, COD, nitrate, fluoride, iron, and coliform to compute a standardized Water Quality Index (WQI). This alerts communities of dangerous contamination risks.

## SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation
- **Target**: Protect and restore water-related ecosystems.
- **Implementation**: The water sources inventory maps reservoirs, lakes, ponds, canals, and groundwater wells using official Survey of India and ISRO Bhuvan sources, monitoring resource volume and seasonal reliability.

## SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities
- **Target**: Reduce the number of deaths and people affected by disasters.
- **Implementation**: The flood susceptibility mapping identifies hazard pooling zones, helping municipal authorities plan safe evacuation routes and construct flood protection infrastructure.

## SDG 13: Climate Action
- **Target**: Strengthen resilience and adaptive capacity to climate-related hazards.
- **Implementation**: Explainable flood susceptibility maps show which factors (elevation, slope, distance to streams) drive risk, providing communities with actionable adaptation data.

## SDG 15: Life on Land
- **Target**: Protect freshwater ecosystems.
- **Implementation**: Tracks forest runoff zones and wetland degradation, preserving land catchments from erosion and contamination.
