# Geospatial Compliance Document

## National Geospatial Policy 2022 Compliance
This project adheres strictly to the **National Geospatial Policy (NGP) 2022** guidelines of India:
1. **FOSS Priority**: Built entirely using Free and Open Source Software (QGIS, GDAL, Python, GeoPandas, Rasterio, FastAPI, Leaflet) preventing vendor lock-in.
2. **Data Availability**: Uses non-restricted base boundaries and public datasets sourced from the official Survey of India portal and ISRO-NRSC Bhuvan.
3. **No Sensitive Infrastructure**: Excludes defense installations, high-resolution national security assets, and sensitive border zones.

## Indian Space Policy 2023 Compliance
Aligning with the **Indian Space Policy 2023**:
1. Uses datasets and platforms hosted and managed by ISRO centers (NRSC, SAC) such as Bhuvan, MOSDAC, VEDAS, and Bhoonidhi.
2. Preserves and separates original source licenses and metadata, detailing clear data lineage.
3. Supports civilian-only applications: public water security, flood warning mapping, and water quality indices.
